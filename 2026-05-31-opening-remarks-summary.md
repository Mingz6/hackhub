## Theme: "Your Second Brain"

> Build AI that helps people **remember, learn, reason, and act** with the knowledge they already have.

Software or hardware welcome. Jen offered hardware mentorship (Raspberry Pi, smart buses).

## Judging Criteria (General Track)

1. **Innovation** — novelty of approach
2. **Technical Execution** — quality of build
3. **Usability** — can people actually use it
4. **Problem Relevance** — does it solve a real pain

**Bonus:** Go-to-market plan (not required but differentiator)

---

## Demo Format

- **2 minutes max** per team — elevator pitch with live demo
- Three things to show:
  1. **Problem** — what hurts?
  2. **Demo** — what did you build? (MUST be live/real, not faked)
  3. **Impact** — why does it matter?
